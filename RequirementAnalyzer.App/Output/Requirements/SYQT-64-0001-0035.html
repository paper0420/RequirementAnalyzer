#SYQT-64-0001-0035#

[Thermal_Management],[AKKS],[Error_Handling]
Test Objective: In the freeze flap detection operation, the debounce process fails and abnormally ends.

Requirements

  • 25259 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    If the frozen flap detection was not successfull an event-debouncing (applicable number of attempts) shall be started.
  • 25260 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    If the debouncing was not successfull an error message shall be generated.
  • 25270 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    As reaction for one or more errors the actuator must be able to go to an applicable position.
