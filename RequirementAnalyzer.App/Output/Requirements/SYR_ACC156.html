  • 2.1.18 SYR_ACC156: Component protection - PFC and LLC shutdown to mitigate internal overvoltage conditions
  • 2.1.18.1 Description
  • 2.1.18.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 2.1.18.1.0-2 PFC OVP early detection resulting in fast shutoff of all PFC- and LLC-stages PFC other errors resulting in fast shutoff of module-individual PFC-stage and all LLC-stages
  • 2.1.18.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 2.1.18.1.0-4 PFC enabled PFC and LLC enabled (Charging active)
  • 2.1.18.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 2.1.18.1.0-6 • Detection of overvoltage condition on PFC output for charging module1, module2 or module3 • Detection of other errors: - LVP_Lx - Board_TSD (Temp) - SUB_EN_Lx - AC_OCP_Lx (x= 1,2,3)
  • 2.1.18.1.0-7 Input data:
  • 2.1.18.1.0-8 PIC_Error_Lx(PFC Over voltage error)
  • 2.1.18.1.0-9 Description of behaviour:
  • 2.1.18.1.0-10 • Fast shut-off of PFC-stages (M_Lx_PFC_EN = Disabled) - All modules in case of OVP early detection - Module individual in case of other errors • Fast shut-off of all LLC-stages (M_DSP_CHG_EN = Disabled) - Global shutoff of all 3 LLC-stages through DSP • Initiate restart-sequence after wait time (300ms)
