#SYQT-01-0003-0004#

[Charging],[Startup_Shutdown],[Startup]
Test Objective: Confirm that CCU wakes up by change of the coupler engaging state.

Requirements

  • 8067 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The "CCU" is wake up entitled (Active Wake-Up).
  • 8072 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The wake up process triggered by the proximity signal has to be started within 500ms after the change of the proximity resistor value.
  • 23331 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The "CCU" must wake up the vehicle when the charge plug is pugged off/on (proximity signal change).
  • 23334 -
    [Not Changed][partly accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The "CCU" must implement a recognition function. - toggeling values of proxy resistor - toggeling charging flap position
  • 16852 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The "CCU" must avoid the wake up of the vehicle by a protection function after detection of the following failures: - toggeling values of proxy resistor - toggeling charging flap position
