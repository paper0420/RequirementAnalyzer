  • 6.1.55 SYR_LvDCDC_F_410: LvDCDC is derating due to maximum HV-voltage limit between 461V and 489V.
  • 6.1.55.1 Description
  • 6.1.55.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 6.1.55.1.0-2 The LvDCDC must be operational with reduced power at a maximum HV-voltage between 461V and 489V.
  • 6.1.55.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 6.1.55.1.0-4 LvDCDC current operating state=Buck, RUNLEVEL >= 30.
  • 6.1.55.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 6.1.55.1.0-6 Measured_U_HV over 460V and lower 490V without a time limit.
  • 6.1.55.1.0-7 Input data:
  • 6.1.55.1.0-8 Target_Operating_State = Buck, Target_U_LV = 14V, Measured_U_LV = 14V Measured_U_HV = 461V to 490V for 1 s. Min_Permitted_HV_Voltage = 200V, Max_Permitted_HV_Power = 3100 W, p_DCDC_CONTINUOUS_I_LV = 221 A, p_DCDC_CONTINUOUS_P_LV = 3100 W, Coolant temperature = 35Â°C
  • 6.1.55.1.0-9 Description of behaviour:
  • 6.1.55.1.0-10 The LvDCDC must be operational with reduced power at HV-DC voltages between 461V and 490V. The operation range voltage level is B2. For HW component protection the output current shall be reduced. A DTC must be stored in the info memory of the CCU.
