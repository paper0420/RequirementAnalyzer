  • 7.4.2.12.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 7.4.2.12.1.0-2 This Use-case will use UDS-conform data transfer of data to be flashed. Before transfer the authentication phase has to be completed successfully. There is no difference between software- and data-transfer in conceptual meaning. The transfer will also include, the mandatory provision of storage resources (memory) and checking of validity of content to be flashed (target addresses, size). All received data will be decrypted/flashed on the fly, there is not latching designed. - RoutineControl EraseMemory - RoutineControl CheckProgrammingDependencies - RequestDownload - RequestTransferExit - TransferData - WriteDataByIdentifier Fingerprint - WriteDataByIdentifier VIN (Vehicle Identification Number)
