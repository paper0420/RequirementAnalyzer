#SYQT-12-0002-0016#

[Charging],[14V_DCDC],[DCDC_Control]
Test Objective: Confirm that returning + B to OFF / ON after LVDC overcurrent

Requirements

  • 14526 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Gleichspannungswandlung" shall prevent the output transfer between high-voltage network and low-voltage network if the "DC/DC" converter function is in the "Standby" operating mode.
  • 18151 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    After a definable time "p_DCDC_HV_CURRENT_MAX_WAIT_TIME", the subfunction "Gleichspannungswandlung" shall deactivate the self-protection mechanism after a deactivation due to a HV overcurrent event and activate the converter again.
  • 18152 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Gleichspannungswandlung" shall ensure that the self-protection can be recovered within a driving cycle in the case of a temporary overcurrent in the HV and LV range.
  • 18154 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    An "Error" state that arises due to multiple overcurrent events shall be recovered by a terminal change.
  • 18174 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Fehlermanagement" shall ensure that a failure of the DC/DC converter always leads to a corresponding error memory entry.
  • 18175 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Fehlermanagement" shall ensure that an error memory entry is also set if the DC/DC converter does not supply any output power even though this should be provided.
