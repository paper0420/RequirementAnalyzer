Notes for common configurable Limits: for 3.5kW it is provided 16.5A for 7.0kW it is provided 32.5A for 11kW it is provided 51.5A DTC Codes: Bauteilschutz - DC Überstrom Modul 1: 0x318E6 Modul 2: 0x318EC Modul 3: 0x318F4
