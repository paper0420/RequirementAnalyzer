  • 3.1.54 SYR_HMG_117: [S5, WJ] Inhibition of WJ and S5 Plausibility Errors
  • 3.1.54.1 Description
  • 3.1.54.1.0-1 Brief description: The error condtitions which trigger DTC 0xBA0008 (“Component cooling, temperature sensor: plausibility fault”) shall be inhibited in case of range- or plausibility-errors of the S5 or WJ temperature sensors.
  • 3.1.54.1.0-2 Preconditions: Kl15 ON.
  • 3.1.54.1.0-3 Trigger: ECU Startup.
  • 3.1.54.1.0-4 Input data: See desccription of behavior.
  • 3.1.54.1.0-5 Description of behaviour: Info: Error-condition (1) or Error-condition(2) may trigger the DTC 0xBA0008. - Error-condition (1) ‘WJ SW vs. HW Plausibility’ shall be inhibited by: WJ_OOR_high, WJ_OOR_low - Error-condition (2) ‘WJ vs. S5 Plausibility’ shall be inhibited/reset by: WJ_OOR_high, WJ_OOR_low, S5_OOR_high, S5_OOR_low, S5_plausi_error Releated DTCs for reference: 0xBA0006 (WJ_OOR_high) 0xBA0007 (WJ_OOR_low) 0x2241C0 (S5_OOR_high) 0x2241C1 (S5_OOR_low) 0x2241C4 (S5_plausi_error)
  • 3.1.54.1.0-6 Timing Requirements: See description of behaviour.
  • 3.1.54.1.0-7 Output data: Error-condition (1): g_cmnmeasint_Error_Pack_Coolant_Temp_Sensor.plausibility_err Error-condition(2): g_thmgmtint_Error_Pack_S5Temp.plausibility_coolant
  • 3.1.54.1.0-8 Postconditions: NA
