SYR_LvDCDC_F_290


  • 6.1.31 SYR_LvDCDC_F_290: Derated continuous power at an actual LV range of 14V to 15,5V due to coolant temperature between 71°C and 80°C.
  • 6.1.31.1 Description
  • 6.1.31.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 6.1.31.1.0-2 Outside the normal temperature range the LvDCDC reduces the output current and output power linear.
  • 6.1.31.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 6.1.31.1.0-4 Current_Operating_State=Buck or Standby, RUNLEVEL >= 30, No error
  • 6.1.31.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 6.1.31.1.0-6 Target_Operating_State = Buck, Target_U_LV = 15V, The electronic DC load power is adjusted to 2702W and lower. The electronic DC load current is adjusted to 193A and lower. The electronic DC load voltage is adjusted to 15V. The regulation mode of the electronic DC load shall be constant power. The coolant temperature shall be adjusted from 71°C to 80°C within ca. 5K steps. The next step shall be done until the coolant plate reaches the adjusted temperature during operation.
  • 6.1.31.1.0-7 Input data:
  • 6.1.31.1.0-8 Target_Operating_State = Buck, Target_U_LV = 15V, Measured_U_LV = 14V Measured_U_HV = 380V Min_Permitted_HV_Voltage = 300V, Max_Permitted_HV_Power (Leistung_DCDC-Wandler_Hochvoltteil_maximal_Grenze) = 3905 W, p_DCDC_CONTINUOUS_I_LV = 254A, p_DCDC_CONTINUOUS_P_LV = 3550W Coolant temperature = 71°C to 80°C (stepwise 5K)
  • 6.1.31.1.0-9 Description of behaviour:
  • 6.1.31.1.0-10 Outside the normal coolant temperatures, the output power shall be derated linear according between the specified power of 2702W at 70°C to 0 W at 80°C.
