#SYQT-07-0001-0004#

[Charging],[AC_Charging],[AC_Efficiency]
Test Objective: Efficiency confirmation of single phase in case of HVDC current is 1 A (single phase L1)

Requirements

  • 24633 -
    [Not Changed][partly accepted][HWT (ENG.7/8)][Requirement]
    With a DC current of 1 A the charging device must provide an efficiency of at least 84 %. Boundary conditions: • DC voltage: 330 to 460 V • AC voltage: 230 to 240 V • Supply frequency: 50 to 60 Hz • Inlet temperature: 45 °C • Volume flow: 2 l/min
  • 14158 -
    [Not Changed][partly accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    With a DC current of 1 A the charging device must provide an efficiency of at least 84 %. Boundary conditions: • DC voltage: 330 to 460 V • AC voltage: 230 to 240 V • Supply frequency: 50 to 60 Hz • Inlet temperature: 45 °C • Volume flow: 2 l/min
  • 18452 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    The degree of efficiency of the corresponding charging module shall include the total power loss from AC input to DC output, which shall be ensured by means of continuous evaluation of the sensor values for DC current/voltage and AC current/voltage so that the specified update time of 10 ms is achieved.
