[Charging],[Component_Protection],[Derating]
Test Objective: Cofirm that derated power is output when HVDC output starts from the high temperature state (where the derating is needed). (Variant:11kW with full-load charging)

Requirements

  • 18386 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
