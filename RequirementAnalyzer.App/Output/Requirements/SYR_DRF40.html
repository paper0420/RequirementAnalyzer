  • 4.2.1.3.3.0-1 Kiyosawa-san [18.04.2017] : Which fault should be detected by this signal ? According to the our HW spec, HW diagnostic can detect short to ground and short to battery of power supply. (But we cannot distinguish short to ground and short to battery.) Is it same as BMW expectation ? Lokesh Devaraj [19.04.2017] : Any electrical fault in the CCU system for Acceleration pedal sensor 1 and 2 is detected by Fps_HW_Diag_MCU signal. BMW expectation is to only provide DTC which represents the hardware electrical faults for each pedal sensor 1 and 2. We shall be handling this requirement along with signal qualifiers. Distinguishing cases of S2G, S2B is handled by BMW Application sw. Kiyosawa-san [24.04.2017]: About diagnosis signal (FPs_HW_Diag_MCU), convert ADC to physical is mandatory ? Our HW spec has also ADC thresholds. So we can detect the fault by ADC value. Should we convert ADC of all sensor to physical ? Lokesh Devaraj [24.04.2017] : If fault can be detected with the ADC values alone, then I advise ADC to physical conversion for HW diagnosis signal is not mandatory. About conversion of ADC of all sensors to physical: the expectation from BMW application for interfaces from RTE is as datatype uint16, resolution : 0,00488V. Hence, conversion is required for pedal sensor position signals.
