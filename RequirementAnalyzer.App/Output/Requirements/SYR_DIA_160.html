  • 7.5.1.16 SYR_DIA_160: handle external detected failures
  • 7.5.1.16.1 Description
  • 7.5.1.16.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 7.5.1.16.1.0-2 external devices can misbehave, this use-case will gather and judge all problems caused by external devices (in car or outside car (charging station)). The problems will be judged and combined to result in most usefull DTC generation. Especially follow-up failures needs to be concluded to best, minimum DTC.
  • 7.5.1.16.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 7.5.1.16.1.0-4 external device (in car or outside car) behaves not expected. This could cause follow-up failures of other devices
  • 7.5.1.16.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 7.5.1.16.1.0-6 none
  • 7.5.1.16.1.0-7 Input data: data/no data from other devices
  • 7.5.1.16.1.0-8 Description of behaviour: depending on monitored information from other devices/environment, related DTCs will be set/reset
