#SYQT-12-0001-0004#

[Charging],[14V_DCDC],[DCDC_Control]
Test Objective: Confirmation of low voltage supply limitedoperation (LVDC 8V, 6V)

Requirements

  • 14123 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    During driving the DC/DC converter must provide a maximum current of 100 A for at least 10 s for the low voltage onboard electrical system power supply. The maximum current must be provided not earlier than 3600 s, where the basic current in the meantime is not higher than 50 A. Boundary conditions: LV voltage: 6 V
  • 14124 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    During driving the DC/DC converter must provide a maximum current of 200 A for at least 10 s for the low voltage onboard electrical system power supply. The maximum current must be provided not earlier than 3600 s, where the basic current in the meantime is not higher than 125 A. Boundary conditions: LV voltage: 8 V
