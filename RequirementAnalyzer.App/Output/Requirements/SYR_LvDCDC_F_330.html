SYR_LvDCDC_F_330


  • 6.1.34 SYR_LvDCDC_F_330: Derated power delivery from zero at an actual LV range 10,5V to 14V and Tcool = [-40°C,-26°C]
  • 6.1.34.1 Description
  • 6.1.34.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 6.1.34.1.0-2 Outside the normal temperature range the LvDCDC reduces the output current and output power linear. The linear derating covers the range 0A at minus 40°C to 250A at minus 26°C in the LV voltage range 10,5V to 14V.
  • 6.1.34.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 6.1.34.1.0-4 Current_Operating_State=Buck or Standby, RUNLEVEL >= 30, No error
  • 6.1.34.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 6.1.34.1.0-6 Target_Operating_State = Buck, Target_U_LV = 14V, The electronic DC load power is adjusted to 3556 W The electronic DC load current is adjusted to 250A The electronic DC load voltage is adjusted to 14V. The regulation mode of the electronic DC load shall be constant curent. The coolant temperature shall be adjusted from -26°C to -40°C within 5K steps. The next step shall be done until the coolant plate reaches the adjusted temperature during operation.
