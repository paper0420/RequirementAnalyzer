SYR_GBTDC_14


  • 1.1.29.4 SYR_GBTDC_14: detect low-voltage auxilary power supply pins (A+, A-)
  • 1.1.29.4.1 Description
  • 1.1.29.4.1.0-1 Brief description: Pin38 is connected to 'A+' and Pin39 is connected to 'A-' at I/F Connector. A+ and A- provide low-voltage auxillary power if need to vehicle when plug is connected. If CCU needs off-board charger (EVSE) to provide low voltage auxillary power, then after being energized by the low-voltage auxillary power supply, CCU will judge, through measuring the voltage at A+ whether plug is totally conneted. If CCU doesn't need low-voltage auxillary power provided by off-board charger, then CCU can measure voltage value at A+ to judge where plug is totally connected. If voltage value is 6 V, then CCU will start the cyclic transmission of the communication handshake messages.
