#SYQT-08-0001-0001#

[Charging],[AC_Charging],[AC_Slew_Rate]
Test Objective: HVAC slew rate confirmation(single phase L 1)

Requirements

  • 14108 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The charging device must provide a current gradient of at least 8 A/s in unlimeted operation mode.
  • 17408 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    The charger shall follow changes to the DC target current limits at a minimum control speed of 8 A/s.
  • 11143 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    Each charging module at the DC output shall therefore react to a change in target value at a minimum speed of 8 A/s.
