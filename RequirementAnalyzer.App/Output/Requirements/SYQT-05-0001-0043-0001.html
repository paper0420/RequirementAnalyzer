#SYQT-05-0001-0043-0001#

[Charging],[AC_Charging],[AC_Charging_Control]
Test Objective: Confirm that charging is started in one phase when phase is changed within 2.5s (Charging with left turning grid)

Requirements

  • 23683 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    On the three phase variant during AC charging, any phase error internal to CCU that provokes to disable the phase shall be detected in 2,5 seconds. Note: Internal phase errors are in accordance to CCU design. Examples of internal phase errors are: phase current measurement no available, problems on the switching actuators, phase input contactor stuck open, internal phase path short circuits, etc.
  • 23684 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    On the three AC phases variant during AC charging, in case an internal CCU phase error is detected, then the CCU shall start to charge on one phase within 2,5s.
  • 23685 -
    [Not Changed][partly accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    On the three AC phases 11KW variant, The current on the neutral line shall be less than 18A. FTT: 10000 ms Safe state: Disable the AC charging and inform BMW SW
