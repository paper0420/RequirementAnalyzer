  • 7.10.1.1.128.1 Description
  • 7.10.1.1.128.1.0-1 Brief description:Cho6m embeds two temperature sensors. The first one is triggers automatically FCCU faults in case of over- or under-temperature. If this temperature triggers a fault, the device is in a critical condition. To avoid permanent damage I suggest moving the system to the safe state. The second one is connected to an ADC. The requirement asks to acquire periodically the temperature by software. If the temperature is going too high - or increasing too fast - the software can take the proper reaction. For example, the software could decrease the clock frequency or turn off some peripherals to mitigate the temperature.
