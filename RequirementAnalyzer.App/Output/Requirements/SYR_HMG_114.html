SYR_HMG_114


  • 3.1.51 SYR_HMG_114: Map EWP22/EWP23 LIN-signals to DIDs (UWB)
  • 3.1.51.1 Description
  • 3.1.51.1.0-1 Brief description: EWP22- and EWP23-LIN signals for voltage, current, temperature, setpoint_rpm, current_rpm shall be mapped to corresponding DIDs.
  • 3.1.51.1.0-2 Preconditions: Kl15 ON and LIN Communication between the waterpump EWP22/23 and the LIN-Master CCU is established.
  • 3.1.51.1.0-3 Trigger: Capture freeze frame for DTC 0x224100 - 0x224107 and DTC 0x22410A- 0x224111 as per ZEDIS extract 26.1
  • 3.1.51.1.0-4 Input data: EWP status-information received on LIN-Bus 'H_LIN_4'. Frame 'Status_EWP22_LIN' with signals: U_EWP22_LIN, I_EWP22_LIN, TEMP_EWP22_LIN, ST_TARVL_RPM_EWP22_LIN, ST_RPM_MONI_EWP22_LIN Frame 'Status_EWP23_LIN' with signals: U_EWP23_LIN, I_EWP23_LIN, TEMP_EWP23_LIN, ST_TARVL_RPM_EWP23_LIN, ST_RPM_MONI_EWP23_LIN
  • 3.1.51.1.0-5 Description of behaviour: EWP status-information shall be read by Application-SW (DiagJPSWC). Application-SW shall provide the status information (freeze frame) without any conversion to DEM. Detailed mapping shall be as defined in ZEDIS extract 26.1.
  • 3.1.51.1.0-6 Timing Requirements: NA
  • 3.1.51.1.0-7 Output data: DID mapping as as per ZEDIS extract 26.1
  • 3.1.51.1.0-8 Postconditions: NA
  • 3.1.51.1.0-9 Descriptions of exceptions: NA
  • 3.1.51.1.0-10 Dependencies and interactions: BSW (DEM), PANA Appl (DiagJPSWC), BMW SWC, RTE
