SYR_ACC157


  • 2.1.19 SYR_ACC157: Component protection - DC current limitation during LLC-startup to prevent internal overvoltage conditions
  • 2.1.19.1 Description
  • 2.1.19.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 2.1.19.1.0-2 The output current shall be limited during the startup-phase of the LLC-stages. Backgroud: Unbalance of input voltages is affected by the amount of current at LLC start and potentially causes PFC overvoltage conditions.
  • 2.1.19.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 2.1.19.1.0-4 No charging operation
  • 2.1.19.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 2.1.19.1.0-6 Charging operation is started.
  • 2.1.19.1.0-7 Input data:
  • 2.1.19.1.0-8 Signals HVDC_Current_Lx Calibration • Calib_sle_Timer_hvacMaxCurr_whenChargeStart.0/1/2 • Calib_sle_hvacMaxCurr_whenChargeStart.0/1/2
  • 2.1.19.1.0-9 Description of behaviour:
  • 2.1.19.1.0-10 • Current limitation shall be active for configurable time after LLC startup (Calib_sle_Timer_hvacMaxCurr_whenChargeStart.0/1/2) • Current limit shall configurable (Calib_sle_hvacMaxCurr_whenChargeStart.0/1/2)
  • 2.1.19.1.0-11 Timing Requirements:
  • 2.1.19.1.0-12 As defined by Calib_sle_Timer_hvacMaxCurr_whenChargeStart.0/1/2
