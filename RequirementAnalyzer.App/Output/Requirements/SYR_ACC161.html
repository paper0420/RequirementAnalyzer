  • 2.1.23 SYR_ACC161: Component protection - N-L Swap diagnostic Pass Condition [under construction]
  • 2.1.23.1 Description
  • 2.1.23.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 2.1.23.1.0-2 One of the main root cause of the exploded PFC capacitor issue is a N-L Swap on the EVSE/wallbox diagnosis. In case of CCU failure due to PFC exploded capacitor the setting of a primary DTC is useful to warn the final customer to have the EVSE/Wallbox checked by a qualified electrician.
  • 2.1.23.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 2.1.23.1.0-4 • NL Swap error flag is set
  • 2.1.23.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 2.1.23.1.0-6 • The charging plug is disconnected OR • The switch matrix is in 1-phase mode OR • Voltage unbalance is not present - PFC Voltage unbalance not present AND - AC voltage unbalance not present OR • The PFC voltage is NOT present OR • The AC voltage is NOT present OR • PFC OVP error not present
  • 2.1.23.1.0-7 Input data:
