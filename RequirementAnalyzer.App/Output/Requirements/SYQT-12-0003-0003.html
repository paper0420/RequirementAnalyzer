#SYQT-12-0003-0003#

[Charging],[14V_DCDC],[DCDC_Control]
Test Objective: Confirm that the output current decreases linearly when the LVDC output voltage decreases below 10.5 V

Requirements

  • 6516 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The CCU must be designed for a minimum LV voltage of 6 V for limited operation.
  • 14125 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The current reduction from the value at 10.5V to 200 A at 8 V and 100 A at 6 V must be linear.
  • 14550 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Derating und Schutz" shall reduce the power according to the hardware requirements for component protection reasons if the LV current operating limits are exceeded/undershot. See figure "Derating Behavior Low Power Voltage"
  • 18165 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Derating und Schutz" shall ensure where the LV output current operating limit values are exceeded/undershot that this results in a power reduction as per the specifications from the hardware requirements specification if this is necessary for component protection reasons.
