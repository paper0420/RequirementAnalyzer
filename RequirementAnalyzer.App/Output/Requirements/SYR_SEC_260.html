  • 7.7.1.32 SYR_SEC_260: PMD Key Replacement
  • 7.7.1.32.1 Description
  • 7.7.1.32.1.0-1 Brief description: Number Text Type Obj_001 1. PMD Key Replacement Header Obj_002 The Pana PMD PubKey shall be kept in BTLD space for THS Usecase. req Obj_003 The MF PMD PubKey shall be allocated in SWFL space. req Obj_004 The MF PMD Key shall be updatable by updating the SWFL. req Obj_005 A copy of the MF PMD PubKey shall be made by the Appl before switching to the BTLD, suggested check points are: 1. Appl startup or 2.1 DSC: PMD Readout 0x77DB in Appl 2.2 DSC: 10 02 in Appl req Obj_006 2. UseCase: Old BTLD + New Appl Header Obj_007 The BTLD will use the old PubKey for PMD Readout encryption. req Obj_008 The Appl shall use the new PMD PubKey in Appl space for PMD Readout encryption. req Obj_009 3. UseCase: New BTLD + New Appl Header Obj_010 The Appl shall use the new MF PMD PubKey in Appl space for PMD Readout encryption. req Obj_011 The BTLD shall use the new MF PubKey in HSM for PMD Readout encryption. req Obj_012 4. UseCase: New BTLD + No PubKey in HSM Header Obj_013 The BTLD shall send PMD Readout in plain format. req Obj_014 5. Usecase: THS Software Header Obj_015 The CCU shall use Pana PMD Pubkey in both Appl and BTLD if the unit is in THS state. req Obj_016 The CCU shall identify the THS state based on the Appl's SGBM (THS) AND the initial EcuMode as PlantMode AND Chip's LCS as SD. req
