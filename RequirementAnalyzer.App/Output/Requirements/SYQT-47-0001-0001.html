#SYQT-47-0001-0001#

[Charging],[Mode_Management],[Operating_Mode]
Test Objective: Charger transition confirmation INIT -> Standby -> Precharge -> Precharge Ready -> Charge -> Interrupt -> Charge -> Derating -> Interrupt -> Derating -> Charge

Requirements

  • 22046 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)Review][Information]
    Figure: State transitions with conditions
  • 11159 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Information]
    The following operating states are target operating states and are specified by the function "Ladekoordinator": · Standby · Precharge · Charge
  • 11161 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The high-voltage battery shall be charged only in the "Charge" and "Derating" operating states (the two operating states shall be designated as active states).
  • 11174 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The "Derating" operating state shall be switched to when the output power is reduced in order to prevent damage to the charging electronics hardware.
  • 11175 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    As long as the "Derating" operating state is active, the cause of the power reduction shall be determined and the "LDK" function made available.
  • 17474 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]

  • 11186 -
