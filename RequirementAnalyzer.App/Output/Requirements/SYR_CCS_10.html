  • 1.1.31.14 SYR_CCS_10: Charging setting and schedule renegotiation requested by charging station or CCU
  • 1.1.31.14.1 Description
  • 1.1.31.14.1.0-1 Brief description: In ISO 15118-2, the renegotiation mechanism is used to change charge target settings and scheduling during a charging loop. Renegotiation can be initiated either by the CCU or Charging Station.
  • 1.1.31.14.1.0-2 Preconditions: ISO15118 Communication with Charging station
  • 1.1.31.14.1.0-3 Trigger: To initiate a renegotiation by the CCU, BMW SWC shall trigger BMW_st_RqrtChaCtlCcsInt(5: Scc_ChargingControl_Renegotiation,) To initiate a renegotiation by the Charging station, SCC shall infrom bmw swc by interface BMW_opm_ChaProcChgrCcsISO(2: ReNegotiation) in ChargingStatusRes
  • 1.1.31.14.1.0-4 Input data: Charging Schedule CCU request renegotiation: https://svn-bmw03-pais.eu.panasonic.com/BMW03_CCU_DOC/Common/WorkSpace/ENG.02_SystemRequirementsAnalysis/ENG.02_SystemRequirementSpecification/AdditionalDocuments/Charging_Interface/CCS/Sequence/LH_CCS_ISO15118_AC_EIM_Sequence_EVReNegotiation.pdf Charging station request renegotiation: https://svn-bmw03-pais.eu.panasonic.com/BMW03_CCU_DOC/Common/WorkSpace/ENG.02_SystemRequirementsAnalysis/ENG.02_SystemRequirementSpecification/AdditionalDocuments/Charging_Interface/CCS/Sequence/LH_CCS_ISO15118_AC_EIM_Sequence_EVSEReNegotiation.pdf SOME/IP sequence valid in re-negotiation scenario https://svn-bmw03-pais.eu.panasonic.com/BMW03_CCU_DOC/Common/WorkSpace/ENG.02_SystemRequirementsAnalysis/ENG.02_SystemRequirementSpecification/AdditionalDocuments/Charging_Interface/CCS/Sequence/LH_SOME_IP_CCU_23636.pdf [Enterprise Architect Functional Model] Charging Methods
  • 1.1.31.14.1.0-5 Description of behaviour: To initiate a renegotiation by the Charging station, the charging station has to set EVSENotification in EVSEStatus to 'ReNegotiation'. If so, the CCU shall initiate a renegotiation in a limited time (see also [V2G2-680] [V2G2-689]). To initiate a renegotiation by the CCU, the CCU can decide to perform a renegotiation by sending PowerDeliveryReq with parameter ChargeProgress set to ‘Renegotiate’ (see also [V2G2-510], [V2G2-521], [V2G2-522], and [V2G2-683]. The CCU may renegotiate the charging schedule. e.g. Sequence is describe in Figure I.1 and Figure I.2 of ISO15118.
  • 1.1.31.14.1.0-6 Timing Requirements: As per ISO15118 standard
  • 1.1.31.14.1.0-7 Output data: none
  • 1.1.31.14.1.0-8 Postconditions: Charging Process (communication and energy flow) shall be recalculated as per new charging setting and schedule.
