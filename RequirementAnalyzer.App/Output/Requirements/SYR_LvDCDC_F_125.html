  • 6.1.10.1.0-9 The LV current must be monitored continuously. The current shall be used for plausibility checks. In case that the LvDCDC output current is below 100mA for 5min and the vehicle PWF signal is driving, then a failure message must be sent on the bus. Other checks are wrong current direction, peak currents, output power versus input power, limited minimum currents at lower voltages, zero voltage above 18V.
