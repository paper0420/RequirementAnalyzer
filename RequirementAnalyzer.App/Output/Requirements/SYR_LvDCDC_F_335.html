  • 6.1.39.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 6.1.39.1.0-2 To ensure a voltage greater or equal 10,5V for RCP the LvDCDC shall deliver 350A for 100ms. During the pause of 10s, the output current shall be 170A continuous current or 250A for 10s followed by continuous current of 170A for 50s.

The regulation mode of the electronic DC load shall be constant curent.
