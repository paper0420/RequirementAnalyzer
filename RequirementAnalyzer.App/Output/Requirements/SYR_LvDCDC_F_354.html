  • 6.1.45.1.0-8 The LvDCDC ensures the specified continuous current of minimum 125A at 8V. The current shall be independant from the temperature between [-40°C, 80°C]. At the edges of the temperature range a derating is allowed. The required values are the minimum continuous values.
