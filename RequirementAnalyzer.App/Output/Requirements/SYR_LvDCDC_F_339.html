SYR_LvDCDC_F_339


  • 6.1.42 SYR_LvDCDC_F_339: Derated power delivery to zero at an actual LV range of 10,5V to 14V and Tcool = [71°C, 80°C]
  • 6.1.42.1 Description
  • 6.1.42.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 6.1.42.1.0-2 Outside the normal temperature range the LvDCDC reduces the output current and output power linear. The linear derating covers the range 190A at 71°C and 0A at 80°C in the LV voltage range 10,5V to 14V.
  • 6.1.42.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 6.1.42.1.0-4 Current_Operating_State=Buck or Standby, RUNLEVEL >= 30, No error
  • 6.1.42.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 6.1.42.1.0-6 Target_Operating_State = Buck, Target_U_LV = 14V, The electronic DC load power is adjusted to 2660 W The electronic DC load current is adjusted to 190A The electronic DC load voltage is adjusted to 14V. The regulation mode of the electronic DC load shall be constant curent. The coolant temperature shall be adjusted from 71°C to 80°C within ca. 5K steps. The next step shall be done until the coolant plate reaches the adjusted temperature during operation.
  • 6.1.42.1.0-7 Input data:
  • 6.1.42.1.0-8 Target_Operating_State = Buck, Target_U_LV = 14V, Measured_U_LV = 10V ... 14V Measured_U_HV = 380V Min_Permitted_HV_Voltage = 300V, Max_Permitted_HV_Power (Leistung_DCDC-Wandler_Hochvoltteil_maximal_Grenze) = 3911W p_DCDC_CONTINUOUS_I_LV = 254A, p_DCDC_CONTINUOUS_P_LV = 3911W, Coolant temperature = -26°C to -40°C within 5K steps.
