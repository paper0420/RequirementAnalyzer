  • 4.2.1.9.1.0-10 Any deviation in power supply to acceleration pedal sensors shall lead to deviation in the pedal sensor position values. Hence, the voltage levels of power supply to acceleration pedal sensor 1 and 2 shall be monitored. The monitoring is required to detect the acceleration pedal sensor position signals sensitivity deviations.
