  • 6.1.68.1.0-9 A sensor error will be short circuit to ground, short circuit to the supply voltage, electrical line break, out of range (OOR), End to End (E2E) with Alive-, CRC-, Qualifier check, Timeout errors. The sensor errors must be detected. The error must be communicated per signal and logged as a DTC in the error memory.
