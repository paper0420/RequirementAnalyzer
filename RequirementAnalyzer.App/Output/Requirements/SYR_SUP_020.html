  • 7.4.2.3.1.0-9 Description of behaviour:
  • 7.4.2.3.1.0-10 check the content of flash memory before it's executed. Based on RSA signature
  • 7.4.2.3.1.0-11 Timing Requirements: overall booting time from wake-up-event to normal operation shall not be exceeded
  • 7.4.2.3.1.0-12 Output Data: result of check: TRUE/FALSE
