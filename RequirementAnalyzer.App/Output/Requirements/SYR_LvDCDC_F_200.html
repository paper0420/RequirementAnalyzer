  • 6.1.22 SYR_LvDCDC_F_200: Standby
  • 6.1.22.1 Description
  • 6.1.22.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 6.1.22.1.0-2 After the intialisation the LvSupplySystem is waiting in standby for the request from the BMW power management to deliver energy
  • 6.1.22.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 6.1.22.1.0-4 No error. RUNLEVEL >= 30.
  • 6.1.22.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 6.1.22.1.0-6 The internal startup is finished.
  • 6.1.22.1.0-7 Input data:
  • 6.1.22.1.0-8 RUNLEVEL >= 30
  • 6.1.22.1.0-9 Description of behaviour:
  • 6.1.22.1.0-10 The LvSupplySystem changes itself the operating state Init to Standby, when there is no error detected. The LvSupplySystem shows it's readiness for operation with the signal Current_Operating_State=Standby. No energy will be transferred from the HV to the LV battery. The LvSupplySystem measures HV and LV voltages and sends the information to the HVPM.
