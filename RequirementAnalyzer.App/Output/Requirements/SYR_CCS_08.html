SYR_CCS_08


  • 1.1.31.12 SYR_CCS_08: receive optimised charging schedule from bmw sw
  • 1.1.31.12.1 Description
  • 1.1.31.12.1.0-1 Brief description: During ISO charging communication CCU will negotiate Charging Schedule with external charging station. During the ChargeParameterDiscovery Seq, CCU will share the information with external charging station. This information shall also be provide raw to BMW ECU (e.g. MGU) via Ethernet. When the ChargeParameterDiscovery Res is received over PLC line, the Raw packet shall be identified and provide to BMW ECU (e.g. MGU) for processing. BMW ECU shall create Charging schedule and provide to CCU to be share with external Charging station over PLC (via PowerDelivery) as describe in ISO seq.
  • 1.1.31.12.1.0-2 Preconditions: Ethernet interface shall be up and running. Vector interface to provide Raw packet shall be configure to provide desired packet over Ethernet.
  • 1.1.31.12.1.0-3 Trigger: Seq of ChargeParameterDiscoervery Req/Res
  • 1.1.31.12.1.0-4 Input data: NA
  • 1.1.31.12.1.0-5 Description of behaviour: Refer https://svn-bmw03-pais.eu.panasonic.com/BMW03_CCU_DOC/Common/WorkSpace/ENG.02_SystemRequirementsAnalysis/ENG.02_SystemRequirementSpecification/AdditionalDocuments/Charging_Interface/CCS/Sequence/LH_SOME_IP_CCU_13462_CCU_16475.pdf
  • 1.1.31.12.1.0-6 Timing Requirements: NA
  • 1.1.31.12.1.0-7 Output data: seq with Smart charging
  • 1.1.31.12.1.0-8 Postconditions: charging is possible with charging profile selected by MGU
  • 1.1.31.12.1.0-9 Descriptions of exceptions: timing behavior are not defined by customer, so we have to respect the ISO15118 timing including customer seq while commuincating with differenet ECU
  • 1.1.31.12.1.0-10 Dependencies and interactions: Vector SCC shall provide interface to provide RAW Frames to Ethernet interface. Arxml shall provide interface requested in CCU-13462 and CCU-6499
