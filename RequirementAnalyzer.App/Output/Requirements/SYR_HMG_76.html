SYR_HMG_76


  • 3.1.20 SYR_HMG_76: [AKKS] Additional UWB - Link UWB to Softwaresignal
  • 3.1.20.1 Description
  • 3.1.20.1.0-1 Brief description: AKKS Blockade DTCs (0x224040, 0x224046) shall be associated with new UWB which reflect AKKS target- and current-position as communicated on LIN-bus.
  • 3.1.20.1.0-2 Preconditions: Kl15 ON.
  • 3.1.20.1.0-3 Trigger: Freeze frame to be stored for DTC 0x224040, 0x224046 as per ZEDIS extract v29.
  • 3.1.20.1.0-4 Input data: AKKS1 target- and current position as calculated/received by Thmgmt AKKS2 target- and current position as calculated/received by Thmgmt
  • 3.1.20.1.0-5 Description of behaviour: Map internal signals to DIDs and provide to DEM as follows: - 0xECCE - AKKS_1_SOLLPOS_SCHRITTE - (g_smot_po_akks_lin_Value) - 0xECCF - AKKS_1_ISTPOS_SCHRITTE - (g_st_smot_avl_po_akks_lin_Value) - 0xECD3 - AKKS_2_SOLLPOS_SCHRITTE - (g_smot_po_akks_2_lin_Value) - 0xECD4 - AKKS_2_ISTPOS_SCHRITTE - (g_st_smot_avl_po_akks_2_lin_Value) DID mapping as per ZEDIS extract v29.
  • 3.1.20.1.0-6 Timing Requirements: NA
  • 3.1.20.1.0-7 Output data: DIDs: 0xECCE, 0xECCF, 0xECD6, 0xECD7
  • 3.1.20.1.0-8 Postconditions: NA
  • 3.1.20.1.0-9 Descriptions of exceptions: AKKS1 is not mounted for SP21 car lines.
  • 3.1.20.1.0-10 Dependencies and interactions: BSW (DEM)
