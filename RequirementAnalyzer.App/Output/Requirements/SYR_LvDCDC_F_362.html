  • 6.1.48 SYR_LvDCDC_F_362: Current delivery with at least 50 A continuous current at 6V to 125A at 8V
  • 6.1.48.1 Description
  • 6.1.48.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 6.1.48.1.0-2 Current delivery with at least 50 A continuous current at 6V to 125A at 8V. A limited operation at the edge of the limited temperatures at -40°C and 80°C is allowed.
  • 6.1.48.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 6.1.48.1.0-4 Current_Operating_State=Buck or Standby, RUNLEVEL >= 30, No error

  • 6.1.48.1.0-6 Input data:
  • 6.1.48.1.0-7 Description of behaviour:
  • 6.1.48.1.0-8 The LvDCDC ensures the specified continuous current of minimum 50A at 6V. And the LvDCDC ensures the specified continuous current of minimum 125A at 8V. The current shall be indpenedant from the temperature between [-26°C, 70°C]. Not customer required to the KLH is, that the derating to zero at the extreme temperature limits -40°C and 80°C shall begin at the intersection of this currents with the current curves at the 10,5V voltage level.
