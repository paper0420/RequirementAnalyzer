  • 6.1.46 SYR_LvDCDC_F_356: At 8V ensure peak current of 200A for at least 10s for Tcool [-26°C, 70°C]
  • 6.1.46.1 Description
  • 6.1.46.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 6.1.46.1.0-2 The maximum provided current is 200A at 8V for at least 10s with a reduction to 125A for the following 3600s.
  • 6.1.46.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 6.1.46.1.0-4 Current_Operating_State=Buck or Standby, RUNLEVEL >= 30, No error
  • 6.1.46.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 6.1.46.1.0-6 Target_Operating_State = Buck, Target_U_LV = 14V, The electronic DC load power is adjusted to 1800 W The electronic DC load current is adjusted to 200A for 10s and 125A for 3600s. The electronic DC load voltage is adjusted to 8V. The regulation mode of the electronic DC load shall be constant curent.
  • 6.1.46.1.0-7 Input data:
  • 6.1.46.1.0-8 Target_Operating_State = Buck, Target_U_LV = Measured_U_LV = 8V Measured_U_HV = 380V Min_Permitted_HV_Voltage = 200V, Max_Permitted_HV_Power = 3100 W, p_DCDC_CONTINUOUS_I_LV = 221 A, p_DCDC_CONTINUOUS_P_LV = 3100 W, Coolant temperature = between -26°C and 70°C.
  • 6.1.46.1.0-9 Description of behaviour:
  • 6.1.46.1.0-10 For the availability of the EPS the LvDCDC must supply 200A current at 8V for minimum 10s, followed by 125A for the next 3600s.
