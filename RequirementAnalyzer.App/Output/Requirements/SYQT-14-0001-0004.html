#SYQT-14-0001-0004#

[Charging],[14V_DCDC],[DCDC_Efficiency]
Test Objective: DTC registration when out of LVDC DC efficiency range

Requirements

  • 18128 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The "DC/DC" function shall read in a substitute value for the voltage value on the high-voltage side of the DC/DC converter via the logical signal "Spannung_DCDC-Wandler_Hochvoltteil_Ersatzwert".
  • 18169 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    During the monitoring of the degree of efficiency, the subfunction "Derating und Schutz" shall from a parameterizable deviation "p_DCDC_HIGH_DEVIATION_POWEREFFICIENCY" of the degree of efficiency from a parameterizable nominal value "p_DCDC_NOMINAL_POWEREFFICIENCY" read in the value "high deviation from nominal degree of efficiency" for the logical signal "Fehlerstatus_DCDC-Wandler_Ist".
  • 18170 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    During the monitoring of the degree of efficiency, the subfunction "Derating und Schutz" shall from a parameterizable deviation "p_DCDC_HIGH_DEVIATION_POWEREFFICIENCY" of the degree of efficiency from a parameterizable nominal value "p_DCDC_NOMINAL_POWEREFFICIENCY" set an error memory entry that contains the error type as well as the legally relevant (where MIL is triggered) environmental data.
