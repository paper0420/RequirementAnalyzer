  • 1.1.30.12.1.0-5 Description of behaviour: After PLC chip is ready to communicate and have physical link established. Customer SW can request to establish datalink with charging station. When this request is made customer SW shall also specifiy which procedure to use DC(DIN70121) or AC(ISO15118) for datalink establishment. Tolerance for SLAC attenutation DC Charging (DIN): 0 - 45 dB EVSE_FOUND > 45 dB EVSE_NOT_FOUND ISO AC EIM 15118 is not delivered in G08. AC Charging: 0 - 35 dB EVSE_FOUND 35 - 45 dB EVSE_POTENTIALLY_FOUND > 45 dB EVSE_NOT_FOUND
