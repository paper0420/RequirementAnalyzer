#SYQT-17-0003-0020#

[Charging],[Component_Protection],[Derating]
Test Objective: Confirm that the LVDC output current decreases linearly at WJ temperature derating

Requirements

  • 14059 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The current reduction from 254 A at 35 °C to 243 A at 45 °C and to the 193 A at maximum inlet temperature for unlimited operation must be linear.
  • 6706 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The Entry into the "limited operating mode" should not lead to Entries into the error register. .
  • 9999 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    An error memory entry with the content "DC/DC limitation in driving readiness/terminal 15 via power, current or voltage" shall be generated as soon as the DC/DC converter is limited in vehicle operation due to external influences.
  • 14503 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
  • 14548 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    A derating of the LV output power is permitted if the ambient temperature falls below the value "p_DCDC_MIN_T" or exceeds the value "p_DCDC_MAX_T".
  • 14551 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Derating und Schutz" shall ensure where the DC/DC converter temperature (cooling as well as (if available) component tempatures) operating limit values are exceeded/undershot that this results in a power reduction as per the specifications from the hardware requirements specification if this is necessary for component protection reasons. The derating should in be implemented so that a hard shutdown of the DC/DC converter is avoided. If there are no requirements in the HW-LH for temperature and assigend maximal power, these must be agreed separately with BMW. See figure "Derating Behavior Temperature"
  • 14552 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Derating und Schutz" shall determine a separate minimum value to which the power must be reduced for every derating parameter by means of a derating characteristic curve.
  • 14553 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Derating und Schutz" shall, in turn, form a minimum from the individually determined minimum values and accordingly reduce the power by means of the overall minimum value.
  • 18171 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Derating und Schutz" shall read in information on the existing DC/DC converter derating state for the logical signal "Deratingstatus_DCDC-Wandler_Ist".
  • 14562 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Fehlermanagement" shall enter a secondary error memory entry with the content "DC/DC limitation via power, current or voltage" as soon as the DC/DC converter carries out a derating due to external or internal influences.
  • 14568 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    The subfunction Temperatur Provider" shall determine the critical temperature, that causes derating or deactivation, for the DC/DC converter. Remark: The critical temperature is the temperature value,which corresponds to a location considered critical for the DC/DC converter protection.
  • 18178 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Temperatur Provider" shall determine the coolant temperature at the DC/DC converter cooling connection.
  • 18179 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Temperatur Provider" shall read in the determined coolant temperature value for the logical signal "Temperatur_Cooling_DCDC-Wandler_Ist".
