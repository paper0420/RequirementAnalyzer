#SYQT-18-0001-0001#

[Charging],[Component_Protection],[AC_Overvoltage]
Test Objective: AC overvoltage detection confirmation (Single phase L1)

Requirements

  • 18048 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    An overvoltage of the local low voltage supply is to be signaled with an entry in the error memory.
  • 18393 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The "Komponentenschutz und Derating" function shall detect the following protection conditions (detect errors and monitor limit values) in order to adjust operation of the charging electronics without damaging the hardware: - AC undervoltage - DC-HV undervoltage - AC overvoltage - DC-HV overvoltage - Maximum AC current - Maximum DC-HV current - Hardware fault (e.g. short circuit) - Excess temperature - Broken N-Line
  • 18409 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The function "Komponentenschutz und Derating" shall detect an AC overvoltage error if the charger measures an AC voltage over the maximum AC operating voltage for a parameterizable period.
