  • 6.1.5 SYR_LvDCDC_F_115: LV Voltage monitoring and reaction95
  • 6.1.5.1 Description
  • 6.1.5.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 6.1.5.1.0-2 The LvDCDC shall monitor the LV voltage and react with power regulation to crossings of the voltage thresholds.
  • 6.1.5.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 6.1.5.1.0-4 No error. RUNLEVEL >= 30.
  • 6.1.5.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 6.1.5.1.0-6 None
  • 6.1.5.1.0-7 Input data:

  • 6.1.5.1.1-1 Description of behaviour:
  • 6.1.5.1.1-2 The LV side voltage must be monitored. Performance reactions and notifications on crossing voltage thresholds must be started. The LV voltage thresholds are: below 6V, then 8V, 10,5V, 15,5V, 16V and 18V. The CCU must send overvoltage (OV) and undervoltage (UV) threshold information on the bus.
  • 6.1.5.1.1-3 Timing Requirements:
