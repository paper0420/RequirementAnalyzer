#SYQT-64-0001-0028#

[Thermal_Management],[AKKS],[Diag jobs]
Test Objective: Confirm the operation to set AKKS to the target value at DIAGjob

Requirements

  • 26963 -
    [Not Changed][partly accepted][SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    The lastest version of the tester job is found in Zedis.
  • 25272 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    A tester job shall be provided which sets the actuation on a target value. A interval step of 1% between 0% and 100% shall be implemented. 0% is the applicated closed position, 100% the applicated open position.
  • 25273 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    It shall only be possible to activate the tester job in applicable areas (vehicle status, speed, vehicle speed).
  • 25281 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    The tester job opening / closing shall be implemented individually for EACH actuator.
