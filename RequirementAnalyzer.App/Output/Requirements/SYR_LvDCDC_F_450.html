SYR_LvDCDC_F_450


  • 6.1.58 SYR_LvDCDC_F_450: LvDCDC is derating due to maximium LV limit of 16V
  • 6.1.58.1 Description
  • 6.1.58.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 6.1.58.1.0-2 The LvDCDC must reduce the output current to 0 A if 16 V are exceeded. The LvDCDC shall allow a maximum LV voltage of 16V for 100ms.
  • 6.1.58.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 6.1.58.1.0-4 LvDCDC current operating state=Buck, RUNLEVEL >= 30.
  • 6.1.58.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 6.1.58.1.0-6 Target_Operating_State = Buck, Target_U_LV = 15,5V, The electronic DC load power is adjusted to 3500 W The electronic DC load current is adjusted to The electronic DC load voltage is adjusted to 15,5V and max 16V for <= 0,1s. The regulation mode of the electronic DC load shall be constant curent. The coolant temperature shall be adjusted between -26°C and 70°C.
  • 6.1.58.1.0-7 Input data:
  • 6.1.58.1.0-8 Target_Operating_State = Buck, Target_U_LV = Measured_U_LV = 15,5V p_DCDC_DERAT_MAX_U_LV = 16V p_DCDC_ABSOLUT_MAX_U_LV = 15,5V Measured_U_HV = 380V Min_Permitted_HV_Voltage = 200V, Max_Permitted_HV_Power = 3100 W, p_DCDC_CONTINUOUS_I_LV = 221 A, p_DCDC_CONTINUOUS_P_LV = 3100 W, Coolant temperature = between -26°C and 70°C.
  • 6.1.58.1.0-9 Description of behaviour:
  • 6.1.58.1.0-10 The LvDCDC must reduce the output current to 0 A if the LV voltage is equal or higher than p_DCDC_DERAT_MAX_U_LV. The LvDCDC must not exceed a maximum LV voltage of p_DCDC_DERAT_MAX_U_LV = 16V for more than 100ms, even not at a load reduction. The LvDCDC has not to exceed 18V. An info DTC shall be stored.
  • 6.1.58.1.0-11 Timing Requirements:
  • 6.1.58.1.0-12 Debounce Time = 10ms Debounce count = 10
  • 6.1.58.1.0-13 Output Data:
  • 6.1.58.1.0-14 Current_Operating_State = Buck Measured_LVcurrent= 0A Measured_U_LV = 16V p_DCDC_DERAT_MAX_U_LV = 16V p_DCDC_ABSOLUT_MAX_U_LV = 15,5V Strom_DCDC-Wandler_Niedervoltteil_maximal_verfügbar = as calculated Spannung_DCDCWandler_Niedervoltteil_gemessen_Ist = as measured (16V) Strom_DCDC-Wandler_Hochvoltteil_gemessen_Ist = 0A, Spannung_DCDC-Wandler_Hochvoltteil_gemessen_Ist > 0 V An info DTC for LV derating at overvoltage will be stored.
  • 6.1.58.1.0-15 Postconditions:
  • 6.1.58.1.0-16 Current_Operating_State = Buck Measured_LVcurrent > 0A Measured_U_LV = 16V Strom_DCDC-Wandler_Niedervoltteil_maximal_verfügbar = as calculated An info DTC for LV derating at overvoltage is stored.
