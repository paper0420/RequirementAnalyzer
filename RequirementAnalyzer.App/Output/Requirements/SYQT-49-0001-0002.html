#SYQT-49-0001-0002#

[Charging],[14V_DCDC],[DCDC_Ripple]
Test Objective: LVDC input current ripple measurement

Requirements

  • 9945 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][HWT (ENG.7/8)][Requirement]
    The DC/DC converter HV/LV must not generate a peak current ripple on the HV side exceeding 24 mA when this DC current is lower as 160 mA. Boundary conditions: • Ripple frequency range: 10 Hz to 200 kHz The validation of this requirement must be realized with test requirements according to chapter "Test generated voltage/current ripple".
  • 9946 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][HWT (ENG.7/8)][Requirement]
    The DC/DC converter HV/LV must filter the ripple with a frequency >= 200 Hz when computing and transfering the mean value over time of the current signal. Boudary conditions: Minimal attenuation = -20 dB
  • 6543 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][HWT (ENG.7/8)][Requirement]
    The DC/DC converter HV/LV must filter the ripple with a frequency >= 200 Hz when computing and transfering the mean value over time of the voltage signal. Boudary conditions: • Minimal attenuation = -20 dB
  • 14140 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][HWT (ENG.7/8)][Requirement]
    • A documentation of the test results regarding DC/DC converter HV/LV must be provided including following informations: • A comparison between measurements and the specified ripple limits • The measurement data (Current/Voltage ripple) • The input impedance on the HV-Side in frequency range 0 to 200 kHz
  • 14145 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][HWT (ENG.7/8)][Requirement]
    A documentation of the test results regarding DC/DC converter HV/LV must be provided including following informations: • A comparison between measurements and the specified ripple limits • The measurement data (Current/Voltage ripple) • The input impedance on the HV-Side in frequency range 0 to 200 kHz
  • 9808 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][HWT (ENG.7/8)][Requirement]
    The DC/DC converter HV/LV must not generate a peak current ripple on the HV side exceeding 15 % of the actual DC current on the HV side of the DC/DC converter HV/LV. Boundary conditions: • Ripple frequency range: 10 Hz to 200 kHz • DC current: > 160 mA The validation of this requirement must be realized with test requirements according to chapter "Test generated voltage/current ripple".
  • 14506 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Zustandsmanagement" serves for managing the operating states of the "DC/DC" function: "Init", "Standby", "Buck", "Error" and "Crash". The respective active states are communicated to other functions. The respective operating mode adopted by the subfunction "Zustandsmanagement" corresponds with the operating mode of the "DC/DC" function.
