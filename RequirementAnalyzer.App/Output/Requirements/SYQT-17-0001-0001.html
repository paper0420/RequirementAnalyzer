#SYQT-17-0001-0001#

[Charging],[Component_Protection],[Derating]
Test Objective: Cofirm that the derating of HVDC output power works correctly by temperature rise. (Variant:11kW with full-load charging)

Requirements

  • 18382 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The "Komponentenschutz und Derating" function shall detect the following derating condition (detect errors and monitor limit values) in order to provide the maximum available output power without damaging the hardware: - Excess temperature
  • 18383 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The "Komponentenschutz und Derating" function shall trigger an operating mode change to "Derating" state when a derating condition is present.
  • 18384 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The "Komponentenschutz und Derating" function shall calculate the maximum available DC-HV power such that damage to the charging electronics is prevented.
  • 18385 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The derating power per charging module shall not fall below the maximum output value (e.g. 3500 W DC power) until the temperature limit specified by the hardware requirements specification (e.g. 70°C) up to which the charging module shall achieve full charging power has been exceeded.
  • 18386 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    If the temperature up to which the charging module shall not derate is exceeded, the derating power shall correspond to the maximum output value that the hardware can still provide.
  • 18394 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SWQT (ENG.8)][Information]
    The respective threshold values for triggering the protection conditions shall be designed in accordance with the applicable requirements on the hardware used.
  • 18441 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    Power shall be provided at the HV output of the charging modules only in the "Charging" and "Derating" operating states.
