#SYQT-37-0010-0008#

[Charging],[DC_Charging],[DC_Charging_Control]
Test Objective: Assembly mode The status of "GBT A+" is transmitted by "GBT A+_Sts" and the status of "GBT A-" is transmitted by "GBT A-_ Sts".

Requirements

  • 25350 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)Review][Requirement]
    The mean value(over last 10ms) of the E_U_GBT_A+ shall be sent over signal "GBT_A+_Sts".
  • 25351 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)Review][Requirement]
    The mean value(over last 10ms) of the E_U_GBT_A- shall be sent over signal "GBT_A-_Sts".
  • 7536 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    In the inactive state the assembly mode shall not affect the control unit functionality.
  • 24906 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The supplier function shall detect the status of the inputs A+/A- (AuxPower) and send the status to the "Ladeschnittstelle" function. Notes: • The power supply function of the inputs according to the standard is not used. • A digital acquisition of the inputs is sufficient.
