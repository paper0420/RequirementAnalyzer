  • 2.1.20 SYR_ACC158: Component protection - N-Line disconnect diagnostic Fail Condition [under construction]
  • 2.1.20.1 Description
  • 2.1.20.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 2.1.20.1.0-2 One of the main root cause of the exploded PFC capacitor issue is a N-line disconnection on the EVSE/wallbox diagnosis. In case of CCU failure due to PFC exploded capacitor the setting of a primary DTC is useful to warn the final customer to have the EVSE/wallbox checked by a qualified electrician.
  • 2.1.20.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 2.1.20.1.0-4 • The charging plug is connected AND • the S2 switched is closed AND • The switch matrix is in 3-phase mode AND • Bit 1 of g_calib_sle_DebugFlag_NLineDisConn = 0 (Enable) AND • The PFC voltage is present AND • The AC voltage is present AND • PFC OVP error flag set AND • NL Swap error flag is not set for any line AND • at least one of the module is commanded to charge state
  • 2.1.20.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 2.1.20.1.0-6 • PFC Voltage unbalance OR • AC voltage unbalance
