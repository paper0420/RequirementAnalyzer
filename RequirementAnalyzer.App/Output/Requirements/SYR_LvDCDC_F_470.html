  • 6.1.62.1.0-9 Description of behaviour:
  • 6.1.62.1.0-10 The LvDCDC shall assure by SW regulation or by hardware, that the HV-DC current consumption is not higher than 20A continuously. Based on a efficiency of the LvDCDC of 90 percent, the 20A can be fulfilled for the continuous amount of power of (3550W / 0,9 ) at 199V. The expected peak power of (4050W/0,9) for 10s will increase the current up to 22,6A for 10s. But together with the pause time of 50s granted by the CCU KLH specification, a mean value of 20A and lower can be fulfilled in the normal operating range for HV down to 200V.

  • 6.1.62.2 Differences to CRS
  • 6.1.62.2-1 The LvDCDC shall deliver peak power of 4050W for 10s. With respect of a minimum efficiency of 90% the input peak power is about 4500W. And at 199V HV voltage the input current will be 22,6A for 10s. And there would be no problem at the worst case condition, at least for 11KW variant for short period of time. For understanding, the 20A is calculated based on the continuous amount of power of (3550W / 0,9 ) at 199V.
