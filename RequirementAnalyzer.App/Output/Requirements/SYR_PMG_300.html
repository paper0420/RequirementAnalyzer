SYR_PMG_300


  • 7.2.1.27 SYR_PMG_300: RSU update power support
  • 7.2.1.27.1 Description
  • 7.2.1.27.1.0-1 Brief description: while RSU is ongoing, the CCU shall supply the 12V board-net with energy from HV-battery. By this the CCU shall not participate in SW-Update on request of RSU-Master-device. See https://svn-bmw03-pais.eu.panasonic.com/BMW03_CCU_DOC/Common/WorkSpace/ENG.03_SystemArchitecturalDesign/ENG.03_SYAD_Design/RSU_SUPPORT/description.pdf
  • 7.2.1.27.1.0-2 Preconditions: RSU update is ongoing
  • 7.2.1.27.1.0-3 Trigger: request from RSU-Master
  • 7.2.1.27.1.0-4 Input data: indication of RSU-Mode yes/no
  • 7.2.1.27.1.0-5 Description of behaviour: CCU will operate DC/DC converter, even not requested via CAN (while requesting device is updating it's software)
  • 7.2.1.27.1.0-6 Timing Requirements: none
  • 7.2.1.27.1.0-7 Output data: none (just energy)
