#SYQT-35-0001-0003-0003#

[Device_Control],[E_FAN],[FAN_SEV]
Test Objective: During DCDC charge,If the LVDC voltage is 9V (U_Thr3) or more during after startup(Sleep->Wakeup), E-FAN Relay=ON, otherwise E-FAN Relay=OFF. Also, if the LVDC voltage is 10.5V or higher for 20s when E-FAN Relay=OFF, output the FAN Switch ON signal and confirm that E-FAN Relay=ON.

Requirements

  • 28652 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The E-Fan Control Function is responsible to deactivate the E-Fan depending on the LvDCDC output voltage. The Function performs the following tasks 1. Measure the Output Voltage of the LvDCDC in ASIL B 2. If the Voltage drops below U_Thr1, a degradation to 0A shall be requested in QM 3. If the Voltage drops below U_Thr2, a deactivation of the E-Fan via the relay driver shall be requested in ASIL B 4. A re-activation of the E-Fan is only allowed when the Voltage exceeds U_thr3 5. A diganosis shall be performed on the E-Fan Deactivation to detect any errors with the shut-off path
  • 28660 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The Function Block SEV-CTRL shall stop the degradation or the deactivation of E-Fan, only when the Voltage between B+ and B- is >10,5 V (U_Thr3) for a continuous period of 20s (T1). Note: Tolerance for the Voltage measurement shall be considered. The Voltage between B+ and B- is the Output voltage of the LvDCDC Calibratable Range: 8,5 V- 11 V, Step size 0,1 V Safe State: disconnection of the load supply of the E-Fan has been requested
