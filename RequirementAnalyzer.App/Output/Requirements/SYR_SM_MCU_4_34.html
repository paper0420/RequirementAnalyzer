  • 7.10.1.1.125.1.0-1 Brief description:To ensure safe usage of modules which do not exist redundantly and are connected to only one PBRIDGE(e), one of the following shall be true for each user-visible (via the PBRIDGEn) register: (1) the register is not relevant for the safety goal of the application; (2) the register has a constant value (typically a configuration register) which is periodically checked for correct value (for example, by CRCing); (3) wrong values written into the register are detected by other HW/SW safety measures .
