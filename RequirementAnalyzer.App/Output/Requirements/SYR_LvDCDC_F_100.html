  • 6.1.2.1.0-9 Description of behaviour:
  • 6.1.2.1.0-10 Within a startup sequence plan for the complete CCU the LVSupplySystem wakes up. The LVSupplySystem initialise its SW and HW independantly [14507]. A plausibility check of it's own sensors will be done. If there is no error detected, the LvSupplySystem stays in the state init. No energy will be transferred from the HV to the LV battery.
