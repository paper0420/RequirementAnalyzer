#SYQT-48-0001-0006#

[Charging],[AC_Charging],[AC_Ripple]
Test Objective: Confirm that HVDC maximum output current ripple is within specified value (three phases)

Requirements

  • 14088 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The charging device must not generate a peak current ripple exceeding 25 % of the actual DC current in the charging device and must be <= 6 A. Boundary conditions: • Ripple frequency range: 10 Hz to 200 kHz • DC current: > 1 A The validation of this requirement must be realized with test requirements according to chapter "Test generated voltage/current ripple".
