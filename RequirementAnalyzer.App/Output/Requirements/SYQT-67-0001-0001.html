#SYQT-67-0001-0001#

[Device_Control],[Pedal],[Power_Control]
Test Objective: Confirm that power control of accelerator pedal.

Requirements

  • 24581 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    The CCU shall switch on or switch off the power supply of the acceleration pedal module according to BMW command through CDD_TurnOnOffVccAcpd signal (10ms signal): - If CDD_TurnOnOffVccAcpd is equal to “ON”, then switch on the acceleration pedal power supply. - If CDD_TurnOnOffVccAcpd is equal to “OFF”, then switch off the acceleration pedal power supply. Safe State: The power supply of the acceleration pedal module is switched off FTT: 30 ms
