SYR_LvDCDC_F_610


  • 6.1.80 SYR_LvDCDC_F_610: Check Efficiency deviation and sending a DTC
  • 6.1.80.1 Description
  • 6.1.80.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 6.1.80.1.0-2 The LvDCDC shall monitor the degree of efficiency in "Buck" mode for low efficiency in a certain LV output current range. If the deviation of efficiency is lower than a calibratable threshold, then a DTC and the error status shall be set.
  • 6.1.80.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 6.1.80.1.0-4 Current_Operating_State = Buck; p_DCDC_POWEREFFICIENCY_LV_CURRENT_MIN <= Measured_I_LV <= p_DCDC_POWEREFFICIENCY_LV_CURRENT_MAX
  • 6.1.80.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 6.1.80.1.0-6 Efficiency_LvDCDC < p_DCDC_NOMINAL_POWEREFFICIENCY -p_DCDC_HIGH_DEVIATION_POWEREFFICIENCY
  • 6.1.80.1.0-7 Input data:
  • 6.1.80.1.0-8 Efficiency_LvDCDC
  • 6.1.80.1.0-9 Description of behaviour:
  • 6.1.80.1.0-10 In the range of the LV current between p_DCDC_POWEREFFICIENCY_LV_CURRENT_MIN and p_DCDC_POWEREFFICIENCY_LV_CURRENT_MAX, the LvDCDC shall check it's efficiency. If the difference between the p_DCDC_NOMINAL_POWEREFFICIENCY and the measured efficiency is greater than p_DCDC_HIGH_DEVIATION_POWEREFFICIENCY, then LvDCDC launches a DTC and sets the Fehlerstatus_DCDC-Wandler_Ist accordingly.
  • 6.1.80.1.0-11 Timing Requirements:
  • 6.1.80.1.0-12 10ms to 100ms
  • 6.1.80.1.0-13 Output data:
  • 6.1.80.1.0-14 A DTC that contains the error type, the efficiency value and the environmental data with HV and LV values. Fehlerstatus_DCDC-Wandler_Ist = tbd. Deratingstatus_DCDC_Wandler_Ist = tbd.
