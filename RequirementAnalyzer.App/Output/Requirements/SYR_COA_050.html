  • 7.9.1.9.1.0-2 This UC gives brief description for the Class 2 signal quality signals with Signal Qualifier only. Signal Qualifier represents the application monitoring status. It basically gives the application information of highest, medium, simple and limited signal quality.During partial network on or debouncing COM status in not present in Signal Qualifier in this case Internal Qualifier is needed for evaluation. The Signal Qualifier of the corresponding signal are transmitted and received over the board network.
