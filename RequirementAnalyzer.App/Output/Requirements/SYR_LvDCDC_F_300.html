  • 6.1.32.1.0-2 The LvDCDC detects that max allowed HW power is the reason for derating, derating bit shall be set accordingly.

  • 6.1.32.1.1-4 Setting the derating bit g_dcdcrlt_derating_status (Bit 0) and g_dcdcint_FDetect_Result_HV_Power.derating flag: g_dcdcrlt_available_lv_current == Limited_LVcurrent_byHVPower Above condition shall not apply when: Limited_LVcurrent_byHVPower == p_DCDC_PEAK_I_LV (+-1A) Where, p_DCDC_PEAK_I_LV = 307A Resetting the derating bit g_dcdcrlt_derating_status (Bit 0) and g_dcdcint_FDetect_Result_HV_Power.derating flag:g_dcdcint_FDetect_Result_HV_Power.derating: g_dcdcrlt_available_lv_current != Limited_LVcurrent_byHVPower
