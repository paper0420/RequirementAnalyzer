#SYQT-13-0003-0001#

[Charging],[14V_DCDC],[DCDC_Measurement_Accuracy]
Test Objective: Confirmation of LV voltage measurement value (Standalone start)

Requirements

  • 6115 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The 12V output voltage of the DCDC converter has to be set in the range from 11V to 15.5V with 0.05V steps
  • 6516 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The CCU must be designed for a minimum LV voltage of 6 V for limited operation.
  • 6517 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The CCU must not exceed a maximum LV voltage of 16 V.
  • 18130 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][Review][Requirement]
    Note: In coordination with BMW, the supplier shall accordingly make available additional output signals that also prove necessary over the course of development.
  • 18241 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The "DC/DC" function (DC/DC conversion) shall comply with the values described in the Table "Accuracy parameters for output variables" to ensure the accuracy of the output variables.
