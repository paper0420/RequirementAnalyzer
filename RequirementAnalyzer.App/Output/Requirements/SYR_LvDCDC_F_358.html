  • 6.1.47.1.0-7 Description of behaviour:
  • 6.1.47.1.0-8 The strong reduction of current is allowed from 193A at 70°C, 190A at 71°C to zero at 80°C for all Lv voltages between 10,5V and 14V. At the coolant temperatures above 80°C the LvDCDC shall not deliver current and power. At higher LV voltages the current is lower anyway according to 2,7kW continuous power. At lower LV voltages the requested continuous current is expected to be lower anyway.
