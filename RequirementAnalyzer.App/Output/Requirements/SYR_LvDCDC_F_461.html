  • 6.1.61.1.0-7 The LvDCDC must recover from LV overvoltage error and switch the current operating state to standby. If the voltage goes below the nominal upper voltage threshold of 15,5V +/- 200mV the LvDCDC must control with consideration of Voltagedynamics- and control tolerance- specifications, to the commanded voltage not later than 1 ms. But not before the LvDCDC is beeing commanded to go into buck mode.
