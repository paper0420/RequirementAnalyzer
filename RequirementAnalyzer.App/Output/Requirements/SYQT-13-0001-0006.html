#SYQT-13-0001-0006#

[Charging],[14V_DCDC],[DCDC_Measurement_Accuracy]
Test Objective: Check the value of LVDC power reserve

Requirements

  • 18159 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Gleichspannungswandlung" shall determine the available low-voltage power reserve of the DC/DC converter from the difference between the minimum power limitation and the actual power on the low-voltage side.
  • 18160 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Gleichspannungswandlung" shall read in the value of the determined LV power reserve for the logical signal "Leistung_Reserve_DCDC-Wandler_Ist".
