  • 6.1.67.1.0-9 Description of behaviour:
  • 6.1.67.1.0-10 The LvDCDC shall trigger the self-protection mechanism if the high-voltage currents exceed value p_DCDC_HV_CURRENT_MAX_ALLOWED and can damage the DC/DC converter. After a definable time, the LvDCDC shall deactivate the self-protection mechanism after a deac-tivation due to a HV overcurrent event and activate the converter again. According to the KLH [14562] a secondary DTC shall be stored. As long as the counter p_DCDC_REPEAT_CURRENT_HIGH_CHECK is not exceeded the LvDCC shall not switch into the error state. The a primary DTC shall be stored.
