TARVL_V_FAN_1_LIN ---> target speed value of the eFAN in percentage (linked to BMW_n_FanSp ) b. TARVL_T_FAN_1_LIN ---> indipendent speed value of the eFAN in percentage (linked to BMW_st_FanOpmSp ) c. TAR_OPMO_FAN_1_LIN ---> target modus of the eFAN (linked to BMW_ti_FanSp)

  • 3.1.24.1.0-7 Output data: LIN Message CTR_FAN_LIN

  • 3.1.24.1.0-9 Descriptions of exceptions: 1.TARVL_V_FAN_1_LIN=0xFF (signal not valid) 2.TARVL_T_FAN_1_LIN=0xFF (signal not valid) 3. TAR_OPMO_FAN_1_LIN=0x07 (signal not valid)
