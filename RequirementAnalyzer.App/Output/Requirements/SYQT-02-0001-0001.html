#SYQT-02-0001-0001#

[Charging],[Startup_Shutdown],[Shutdown]
Test Objective: Confirm that CCU goes to sleep regardless of the engagin state of the charging coupler.

Requirements

  • 6255 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    Even if coding did not occur, a control unit shall fulfill its quiescent current requirements. A missing coding shall especially not lead to an increased quiescent current, keeping the vehicle awake or to waking up the onboard electrical system.
