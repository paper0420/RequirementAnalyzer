  • 7.2.1.31 SYR_PMG_340: Power Down Service 11 04
  • 7.2.1.31.1 Description
  • 7.2.1.31.1.0-1 Brief description: The 11 04 is a Power Down service for production and dealer organization , invoked by the tester to measure the quiescent current and is to be implemented by the CCU
  • 7.2.1.31.1.0-2 Preconditions: CCU is operational and PWF State in "PAD" or T15 on.
  • 7.2.1.31.1.0-3 Trigger: Power Down service 11 04 request from the Tester
  • 7.2.1.31.1.0-4 Input data: none
  • 7.2.1.31.1.0-5 Description of behaviour: The service is processed only in the PWF state "PAD" or T15 on, and when the High Voltage is Inactive. The CCU would provide a negative response to the tester in case the PWF state and the High voltage are not in the expected state , in case the conditions are satisfied, the CCU would do a transistion to PWF state "Parken" and then within the next 5 second would stop sending the NM3 message , and do a transition to the passive communication , The BDC would then co ordinate the transition to sleep within the expected time frame of 20sec , which is window time to measure the quiescent current.
  • 7.2.1.31.1.0-6 Timing Requirements: The CCU must do a transition to PWF state "Parken", and stop transmitting the NM3 message in 5 sec
  • 7.2.1.31.1.0-7 Output data: none
  • 7.2.1.31.1.0-8 Postconditions: none
  • 7.2.1.31.1.0-9 Descriptions of exceptions: none
  • 7.2.1.31.1.0-10 Dependencies and interactions: DiagSwC , BswM.
