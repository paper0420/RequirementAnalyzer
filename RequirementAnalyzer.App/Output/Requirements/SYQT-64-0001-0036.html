#SYQT-64-0001-0036#

[Thermal_Management],[AKKS],[Error_Handling]
Test Objective: The break-off process should end normally by detecting the blockade in the flap operation with BMW instruction.

Requirements

  • 25171 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The stepper motor position (target position) shall be calculated.
  • 25181 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The stepper motor mode (normal, service, stop) shall be calculated.
  • 25186 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The speed (TURN_V_AKKS_LIN) shall be set in a configurable manner depending on the requirement (stop test OPEN, stop test CLOSED, opening, closing).
  • 25187 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The torque delimiter (SMOT_TORQ_AKKS_LIN) shall be set in a configurable manner depending on the requirement (stop test OPEN, stop test CLOSED, opening, closing).
  • 25262 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    A function shall be provided, which detects a blocked actuator (blocking during the valid travel path) and cyclically starts break-off tests with strike out (maximum number applicable).
  • 25263 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    Strike out means the repeated attempt to reach the detected blockade position. The position shall be approached from a suitable distance to shake the blockade free.
  • 25264 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    The used strike out distance shall be an applicable parameter.
  • 26954 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    A blockage error shall be healed if the target position is reached or overwritten
  • 26955 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SWQT (ENG.8)][Requirement]
    The blockage detection is active in the normal use condiiton (no block test closed or block test open active) in the travel rance >= applicatable closed position and <= applicatable open postion.
