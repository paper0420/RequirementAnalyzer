#SYQT-19-0001-0003#

[Charging],[Component_Protection],[DC_Overvoltage]
Test Objective: Detect overvoltage of HVDC output voltage and stop charging AC -> HV battery (3-phase)

Requirements

  • 23640 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The CCU during the AC charging should ensure that the DC voltage is in the allowed ranges, that is below 540V. For this safety function: - The supplier should measure the DC voltage on LT4 within the specified range and accuracy. - Supplier will evaluate LT4 voltage, if it is higher than the allowed range then shall stop the AC charging.
  • 6325 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The Object of development shall be designed so that all electronic components and constituents of electronic gearshifts positioned between DC HV potentials and the electric vehicle ground are continuously operational B1 for voltages up to the maximum operating voltage(B2 and B3/4) and temporarily (see the section “Overvoltage”) up to the upper high voltage current circuit limit voltage in accordance with the table “High voltage ranges" (ENBN_ PR_2255).
  • 6423 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    Compliance with the high voltage operating condition B3/B4 for overvoltage shall be demonstrated, see table “High voltage operating conditions (ENBN_PR_2277)”.
  • 6432 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    For the test, the test parameters shall be selected according to the table “Test parameters for overvoltage” (ENBN_PR_2730).
  • 18413 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The function "Komponentenschutz und Derating" shall detect a DC overvoltage error if the charger measures a DC voltage over the maximum DC-HV operating voltage for a parameterizable period.
  • 18415 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The diagnostics for DC-HV overvoltage diagnostics shall be performed in every operating state.
