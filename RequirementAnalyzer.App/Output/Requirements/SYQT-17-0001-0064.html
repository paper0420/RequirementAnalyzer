#SYQT-17-0001-0064#

[Charging],[Component_Protection],[Derating]
Test Objective: HVAC Voltage Derating function check(HVAC voltage control, calibration value control)

Requirements

  • 11174 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The "Derating" operating state shall be switched to when the output power is reduced in order to prevent damage to the charging electronics hardware.
  • 18394 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SWQT (ENG.8)][Information]
    The respective threshold values for triggering the protection conditions shall be designed in accordance with the applicable requirements on the hardware used.
