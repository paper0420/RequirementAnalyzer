SYR_LvDCDC_F_364


  • 6.1.49 SYR_LvDCDC_F_364: At 6V ensure peak current of 100A for at least 10s and 50A for the following 3600s
  • 6.1.49.1 Description
  • 6.1.49.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 6.1.49.1.0-2 The maximum provided current is 100A at 6V for at least 10s with a reduction to 50A for the following 3600s.
  • 6.1.49.1.0-3 Preconditions:
  • 6.1.49.1.0-4 Current_Operating_State=Buck or Standby, RUNLEVEL >= 30, No error
  • 6.1.49.1.0-5 Trigger:
  • 6.1.49.1.0-6 Target_Operating_State = Buck, Target_U_LV = 14V, The electronic DC load power is adjusted to 700 W The electronic DC load current is adjusted to 100A for 10s and 50A for 3600s. The electronic DC load voltage is adjusted to 6V. The regulation mode of the electronic DC load shall be constant curent. The coolant temperature shall be adjusted between -26°C and 70°C.
  • 6.1.49.1.0-7 Input data:
  • 6.1.49.1.0-8 Target_Operating_State = Buck, Target_U_LV = Measured_U_LV = 8V Measured_U_HV = 380V Min_Permitted_HV_Voltage = 200V, Max_Permitted_HV_Power = 3100 W, p_DCDC_CONTINUOUS_I_LV = 221 A, p_DCDC_CONTINUOUS_P_LV = 3100 W, Coolant temperature = between -26°C and 70°C.
  • 6.1.49.1.0-9 Description of behaviour:
  • 6.1.49.1.0-10 The LvDCDC must supply 100A current at 6V for minimum 10s, followed by 50A for the next 3600s. Tcool is between [-26°C, 70°C]. If Tcool is lower or higher than the output current could be limited. At -40°C and 80°C the output current will be zero.
