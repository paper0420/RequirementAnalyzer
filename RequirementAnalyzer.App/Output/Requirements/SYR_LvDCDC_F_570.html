  • 6.1.74.1.0-9 Description of behaviour:
  • 6.1.74.1.0-10 At a crash event the LvDCDC shall stop the power transmission from HV to LV. The crash event can be received either as as a bus signal (tbd) or by the KL_30C_sts status signal. This signal is detected by a HW trigger and processed for the LvDCDC. The signal has the advantage, that the LvDCDC is informed before the HV main contactors disconnects HV.
