  • 7.2.1.32 SYR_PMG_350: Power Down Service 11 41
  • 7.2.1.32.1 Description
  • 7.2.1.32.1.0-1 Brief description: The 11 41 is a Power Down Service used by the BDC in case of On Board electrical error.
  • 7.2.1.32.1.0-2 Preconditions: CCU is operational .
  • 7.2.1.32.1.0-3 Trigger: Power Down service 11 41 request from BDC
  • 7.2.1.32.1.0-4 Input data: none
  • 7.2.1.32.1.0-5 Description of behaviour: The service is processed in all the PWF states.Once the Power down is received , and if the CCU is in active state, it does a transition to Passive state and sends a positive response and the BDC would then co ordinate the transition to sleep. In case the CCU is already in Passive state , the response would be suppressed. During the Power down process and transition to PWF > "Parken" would cancel the Power down service request.
