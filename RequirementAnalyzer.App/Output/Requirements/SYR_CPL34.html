SYR_CPL34


  • 1.1.3.5 SYR_CPL34: Overcurrent detected while locking / unlocking
  • 1.1.3.5.1 Description
  • 1.1.3.5.1.0-1 Brief description:
  • 1.1.3.5.1.0-2 When an overcurrent during actuation is detected a short to battery or a short to ground shall be stored. The differentiation between a short to battery and a short to ground shall be done via the diagnostic interface of the H-Bridge. The actuation will end immediately after detecting the fault to protect the vehicle harness. Pana_Actuation_status will be 2 ( for unlocking) or 0 ( for locking) for the entire duration of the actuation. When the error is detected, Pana_Actuation_status shall be 4 (error).
