#SYQT-05-0001-0030#

[Charging],[AC_Charging],[AC_Charging_Control]
Test Objective: When the KL 30 voltage recovers (KL 30 changes from <6 V to> 6 V), AC charging is not activated for the first 500 ms.

Requirements

  • 23901 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    Once KL30 Voltage is restored (KL30 changes from <6V to > 6V) , for the first 500ms, AC Charging shall not be activated and power feed into the HV-DC Circuit (DCDC Converter) shall not occur. Note: After restoring the LV-board power supply, the HV voltage must not be applied directly to the HV electrical system. All HV components must have an appropriate start-up sequenz which is activated by HVPM. Note: DC-DC Converter shall be activated once KL30>=6V. AC-Charging shall be activated once KL30 >=9V.
