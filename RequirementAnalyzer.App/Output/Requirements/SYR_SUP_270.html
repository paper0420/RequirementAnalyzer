  • 7.4.2.28 SYR_SUP_270: HSM v2.7.8.r0 Two Step Update
  • 7.4.2.28.1 Description
  • 7.4.2.28.1.0-1 Brief description: CCU shall use a Special procedure, utilising the BLU, to accomodate the HSM update to v2.7.8.r0. The procedure is a 2 times flashing of the HSM with the bin of v2.7.8.r0. This is needed to adapt the HSM's NVM config area to accomodate the expanded Keyslots of PnC feature.
  • 7.4.2.28.1.0-2 Preconditions: N/A
  • 7.4.2.28.1.0-3 Trigger: PDX flashing (ensuring BLU and HSM SWFK are included)
  • 7.4.2.28.1.0-4 Input data:None.
  • 7.4.2.28.1.0-5 Description of behaviour: Obj_001 HSM Two-Step Update PDX Header Obj_002 A special Update PDX is needed for Meta.2233 info Obj_003 The PDX shall support the following files update: 1. BLU of 039.150.001 2. HSM First-Update of 2.7.8.r0 as a special update. (To set Config Area) 3. Regular SWFL/SWFK/CAF Update. 4. HSM SWFK as Second-Update for D-Flash retention and Configuration. req Obj_004 The 039.150.001 BLU shall have a security version of 6. req Obj_005 The Meta.2233 Appl shall have a security version of 6. req Obj_006 The 039.150.001 BTLD shall have the "HSM Rollback Prevention" version set to the new version of 2.7.8.r0 req Obj_007 The 039.150.001 BTLD shall support the flashing of the HSM SWFK as part of a full PDX. req Obj_008 The CCU shall retain any history Data stored in the HSM post the PDX update. req Obj_009 The CCU shall accommodate an increase of 10 ECDSA 256 key pairs (from 5 to 15) req Obj_010 The CCU shall retain the same above gained functions (Obj_007 /8/9) with the repeated reflash of the PDX. req Obj_011 First-Update Flag Header Obj_012 The 039.150.001 BLU shall include a version check to compare the exsting HSM's version against the internal HSM-bin. req Obj_013 If the existing HSM's security version is >= 2.7.8.r0, then the BLU shall skip the HSM update step and shall be restricted to BTLD update only. req Obj_014 The VersionCheck is added to close the Special behaviour of the PDX post first use. info
  • 7.4.2.28.1.0-6 Timing Requirements:None.
  • 7.4.2.28.1.0-7 Output data: CCU shall have the HSM in v.2.7.8.r0, which can be read via HSMErrorlog DSC.
  • 7.4.2.28.1.0-8 Postconditions:None.
  • 7.4.2.28.1.0-9 Descriptions of exceptions:None.
  • 7.4.2.28.1.0-10 Dependencies and interactions: Dependency on HSM.
