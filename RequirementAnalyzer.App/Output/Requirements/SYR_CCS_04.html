  • 1.1.31.7.1.0-6 Timing Requirements: see ISO15118 DC
  • 1.1.31.7.1.0-7 Output data: PLC framed to EVSE
  • 1.1.31.7.1.0-8 Postconditions: trigger from bmw SWC and Datalink must be established
