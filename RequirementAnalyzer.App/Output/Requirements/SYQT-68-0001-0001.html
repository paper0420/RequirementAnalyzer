#SYQT-68-0001-0001#

[Charging],[DC_Charging],[Proximity]
Test Objective: Confirm that digital judgment of DC proximity terminal is performed correctly.(In CHAdeMO mode)

Requirements

  • 6889 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    In the case where a CHAdeMO plug is needed: The component must be able to check whether the CHAdeMO cable leading to the charging socket is properly connected. NB. This can be achieved for example with a diagnosable 12V jumper.
