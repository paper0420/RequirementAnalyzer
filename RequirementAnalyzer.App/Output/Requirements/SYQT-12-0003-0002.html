#SYQT-12-0003-0002#

[Charging],[14V_DCDC],[DCDC_Control]
Test Objective: Confirm that the output current decreases linearly when the LVDC output voltage decreases below 10.5 V

Requirements

  • 6516 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The CCU must be designed for a minimum LV voltage of 6 V for limited operation.
  • 21983 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The DC/DC converter HV/LV must fullfill the following requirements for the complete limited operation range, see section "Operating range of CCU". Differences in the boundary conditions are mentioned explicitely in the requirement text if necessary.
  • 9802 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The DC/DC converter HV/LV must provide a continuous current of 50 A for the low voltage onboard electrical system power supply. Boundary conditions: LV voltage: 6 V
  • 14126 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The DC/DC converter HV/LV must provide a continuous current of 125 A for the low voltage onboard electrical system power supply. Boundary conditions: LV voltage: 8 V
  • 14127 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The current reduction from the value at 10.5V to 125 A at 8 V and 50 A at 6 V must be linear.
  • 9999 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    An error memory entry with the content "DC/DC limitation in driving readiness/terminal 15Â via power, current or voltage" shall be generated as soon as the DC/DC converter is limited in vehicle operation due to external influences.
  • 14506 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Zustandsmanagement" serves for managing the operating states of the "DC/DC" function: "Init", "Standby", "Buck", "Error" and "Crash". The respective active states are communicated to other functions. The respective operating mode adopted by the subfunction "Zustandsmanagement" corresponds with the operating mode of the "DC/DC" function.
  • 14508 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Information]
  • 14546 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)Review][Information]
    A derating of the LV output power is permitted if the low-voltage value is less than "p_DCDC_MIN_U_LV" or greater than "p_DCDC_MAX_U_LV".
  • 14550 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Derating und Schutz" shall reduce the power according to the hardware requirements for component protection reasons if the LV current operating limits are exceeded/undershot. See figure "Derating Behavior Low Power Voltage"
  • 18165 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Derating und Schutz" shall ensure where the LV output current operating limit values are exceeded/undershot that this results in a power reduction as per the specifications from the hardware requirements specification if this is necessary for component protection reasons.
  • 14552 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Derating und Schutz" shall determine a separate minimum value to which the power must be reduced for every derating parameter by means of a derating characteristic curve.
  • 14553 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)SYIT (ENG.9)][Requirement]
    The subfunction "Derating und Schutz" shall, in turn, form a minimum from the individually determined minimum values and accordingly reduce the power by means of the overall minimum value.
