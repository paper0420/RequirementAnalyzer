#SYQT-05-0001-0029#

[Charging],[AC_Charging],[AC_Charging_Control]
Test Objective: Confirmation of maximum power (Three-phase)

Requirements

  • 66 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The chargin device must support 11 kWAC charging in three-phase operation.
  • 67 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    VARIANT (11kW AC charging): The component "CCU" must integrate three technical modules "AC/DC converter 3,7kW" for the AC current form the charging socket.
  • 9840 -
    [Not Changed][partly accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The component "CCU" must integrate the technical module "switching matrix" for switching from one to three phase charging in the charger.
  • 9841 -
    [Not Changed][partly accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The component "CCU" must recognize, whether a one phase or a three phase charging station is connected to the vehicle to be able to use the maximum available charge power supported by the vehicle.
  • 69 -
    [Not Changed][partly accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The component "CCU" must provide a 3 phase AC interface to the integrated technical module "switching matrix". (interface to charging socket)
  • 13436 -
    [Not Changed][partly accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The component "CCU" must identify by an Sensor, wether it ist connected to a one phase or a three phase charging Station.
  • 13437 -
    [Not Changed][partly accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The three phase charging interface must be devolped for the operation of L1,2,3/N.
  • 14037 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    All operating loads and special events shall be withstood under all electrical loads occurring during operation.
  • 17473 -
    [Not Changed][accepted][SYQT (ENG.10)][Requirement]
    The AC grid shall conduct controlled charging of the high-voltage battery in the "Charge" operating state.
